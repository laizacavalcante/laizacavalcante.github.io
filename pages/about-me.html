About me

Hey there, I’m Laíza Cavalcante!

I’m an Agricultural Engineer passionate about geospatial analysis always looking forward to learning new topics. I graduated as a Master of Science in Agricultural Engineering in Brazil, which I have the opportunity to develop projects to map land use and land cover areas, extract seasonal trends from time-series satellite data, and use drones to monitor farms. Now, I’m currently working at Newe Seguros as a Data Scientist, to offer better insurance products for Brazilian farmers.

My interests range from Geographic Information Science (GIS), remote sensing, privacy and technology, creative writing, and cooking (especially desserts). This blog is dedicated to some of these topics and other things that pop-up in my mind. So, if we have any common interests, contact me!
